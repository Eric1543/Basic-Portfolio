About Me

As the world is becoming more and more technologically connected, I've decided that the next step includes obtaining skills in computer science. In my spare time, I'm a generalist, who's ideal job would be professional student. I would also like to teach what I have learned along the way for whoever my voice might connect with. I have a wide field of interests including Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Computer Science, Writing, and Psychology just to name a few. The world is such an interesting place - if only there were more time...
